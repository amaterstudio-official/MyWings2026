Take to the Skies

Our Vision & Collaboration

A Standard, Not an Add-on

We believe in integration, not isolation. Our goal is close cooperation with leading open-source aircraft developers. We want legendary planes like the A320neo (FBW), A380X, or A330-900 (Headwind) to be native parts of the MyWings core.

Core Systems

Optimized for deep Airbus and Boeing systems. A simulator built from the ground up for complex avionics and detailed hydraulic circuits.

Open Architecture

SDK designed to make aircraft implementation smoother than ever. Every flight model parameter is fully documented.

Community Code

Every line of simulator code is available on GitHub. Transparency is our priority – from wheel physics to cloud rendering.

Under the Hood

Uncompromising Engine

MyWings 2026 runs on a custom rendering core built on Vulkan 1.3, allowing for high performance even on mid-range systems while maintaining photorealistic visuals.

Atmospheric Effects

Dynamic weather based on real METAR and GFS data. Real-time air flow simulation, including thermals and mountain turbulence.

Global Scenery

A combination of satellite imagery and procedurally generated terrain for worldwide coverage with high-detail airports.

Support & Specs

Frequently Asked Questions

Minimum Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • CPU: Intel i3/i5/i7/i9 (4+ cores) / AMD Ryzen 3/5/7
  • RAM: 8 GB (16 GB recommended for VR)
  • GPU: NVIDIA/AMD with Vulkan 1.3 support (min 4GB VRAM)
  • Storage: 25 GB SSD

Joystick Support?

Yes, the simulator fully supports various hardware. With native mapping, just plug and play for most Thrustmaster, Logitech, and Honeycomb models.
